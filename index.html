About

Sachin Tendulkar (born 24 April 1973 in Mumbai) is an Indian former international cricketer, widely regarded as one of the greatest batters of all time. He holds the world record for the most international runs in both Tests and ODIs, and is the only player to score 100 international centuries :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}.
